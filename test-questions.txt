SAMPLE QUESTIONS TO TEST THE CHATBOT
=====================================

BASIC QUESTIONS:
----------------
1. How many days of annual leave do employees get?
2. What is the annual leave entitlement for employees with more than 10 years of service?
3. How many days of sick leave are employees entitled to per year?
4. What is the maternity leave policy?
5. How many days of paternity leave are male employees entitled to?

DETAILED QUESTIONS:
-------------------
6. What is the procedure for requesting annual leave?
7. How far in advance must annual leave be requested?
8. Can unused annual leave be carried forward to the next year?
9. What is the maximum number of annual leave days that can be carried over?
10. Is a medical certificate required for sick leave?

SPECIFIC SCENARIOS:
-------------------
11. What happens if I need to take leave during my probation period?
12. How many days of bereavement leave am I entitled to for the death of a parent?
13. What should I do if I need to take personal leave for an emergency?
14. Can I take unpaid leave? What is the process?
15. How many public holidays does the company observe per year?

PROCEDURAL QUESTIONS:
---------------------
16. How do I check my leave balance?
17. What information do I need to provide when requesting leave?
18. How long does it take for a leave request to be approved?
19. What should I do when returning from a long leave?
20. What happens if I abuse the leave policy?

COMPARISON QUESTIONS:
--------------------
21. What is the difference between annual leave and personal leave?
22. How does sick leave differ from annual leave?
23. What are the leave entitlements for new employees versus long-term employees?

EDGE CASES:
-----------
24. What if a public holiday falls on a weekend?
25. Can I take leave if I have no remaining annual leave days?
26. What documentation is required for extended sick leave?
