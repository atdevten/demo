COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

1. ANNUAL LEAVE (VACATION DAYS)

All full-time employees are entitled to annual leave based on their years of service:
- 0-2 years: 12 days per year
- 3-5 years: 15 days per year
- 6-10 years: 20 days per year
- 11+ years: 25 days per year

Annual leave accrues monthly and can be taken in full days or half days. Employees must submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned absences. Emergency leave requests may be approved with manager discretion.

2. SICK LEAVE

Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per calendar year. Sick leave can be used for:
- Personal illness or injury
- Medical appointments
- Care for immediate family members who are ill

Sick leave does not require advance notice but employees must notify their manager as soon as possible, preferably before the start of their workday. Medical certificates may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. PERSONAL LEAVE

Employees may take up to 5 unpaid personal days per year for personal matters that cannot be scheduled outside work hours. Personal leave requires manager approval and should be requested at least 1 week in advance when possible.

4. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE

Employees are entitled to paid bereavement leave for the death of immediate family members:
- Spouse/Partner: 5 days
- Parent, Child, Sibling: 3 days
- Grandparent, Grandchild: 1 day

Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager discretion.

5. MATERNITY/PATERNITY LEAVE

- Maternity Leave: 12 weeks paid leave for birth mothers
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks paid leave for fathers/partners
- Adoption Leave: 8 weeks paid leave for adoptive parents

Leave must be requested at least 4 weeks before the expected date. Employees may combine leave with annual leave if needed.

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS

The company observes the following public holidays:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day (First Monday in September)
- Thanksgiving (Fourth Thursday in November)
- Christmas Day (December 25)

If a public holiday falls on a weekend, the company will observe it on the nearest weekday.

7. LEAVE REQUEST PROCEDURE

All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal or via email to your direct manager. Requests should include:
- Type of leave
- Start date and end date
- Reason for leave (if applicable)
- Contact information during absence

Managers will respond to leave requests within 3 business days. Approved leave will be recorded in the HR system.

8. CARRYOVER AND EXPIRATION

- Annual leave: Up to 5 days may be carried over to the next year if unused. Remaining days expire on December 31.
- Sick leave: Does not carry over. Unused days expire at year-end.
- Personal leave: Does not carry over.

9. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY

Employees may request unpaid leave for extended absences. Such requests require:
- Written request to HR and direct manager
- Minimum 4 weeks advance notice
- Approval from both manager and HR department
- Maximum 30 days per calendar year

10. EMERGENCY LEAVE

In case of emergencies, employees should contact their manager immediately. Emergency leave may be granted for:
- Medical emergencies
- Family emergencies
- Natural disasters
- Other unforeseen circumstances

Emergency leave will be deducted from available leave balances or may be unpaid if no leave is available.

11. LEAVE BALANCE INQUIRIES

Employees can check their leave balances at any time through:
- HR portal
- Email request to HR department
- Monthly leave balance statements

12. POLICY UPDATES

This policy is reviewed annually and may be updated. Employees will be notified of any changes at least 30 days in advance.

For questions or clarifications, please contact the HR department at hr@company.com or extension 1234.
